What is dental diagnostics?

Dental diagnostics is a complex assessment of the condition of teeth, gums, and oral cavity, allowing to detect diseases at an early stage and plan effective treatment. Accurate diagnostics help avoid complications, ensure successful treatment results, and long-term patient comfort.

When are tests and diagnostics recommended?

Dental tests are recommended when:

General Health

assess general oral health

Noticed Problems

problems are observed - pain, sensitivity, gum swelling

Complex Treatment

complex treatment is planned - implantation, prosthetics, orthodontics

Prevention

aim to avoid diseases or detect them early

Aesthetics

want to ensure an aesthetic and functional smile

Procedure Options

We offer various diagnostic methods ensuring accurate and detailed information.

X-Ray Tests

Assessment of tooth roots, bone, and occlusion condition

  • Accurate root image
  • Minimal radiation dose
  • Fast result

3D Scanning / CBCT

Accurately visible bone structure, sinus, and nerve position

  • 3D visualization
  • Accurate implant planning
  • Full mouth view

Intraoral Scanning

Fast and accurate teeth and occlusion information

  • Contactless scanning
  • Digital impressions
  • 3D modeling

Periodontal Tests

Gum and bone tissue assessment, periodontitis diagnostics

  • Gum pocket measurement
  • Gingivitis detection
  • Treatment planning

Aesthetic Tests

Smile planning, color and shape selection

  • Digital smile design
  • Color analysis
  • Aesthetic planning
