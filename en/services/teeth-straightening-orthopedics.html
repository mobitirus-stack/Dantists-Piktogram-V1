What is Teeth Straightening?

Teeth straightening, also called orthodontic treatment, is a set of procedures and measures for correction of irregularly growing or positioned teeth. Correct tooth position not only improves aesthetics but also facilitates oral hygiene, reduces risk of caries and gum diseases, and ensures proper chewing load.

Teeth straightening is suitable for both children and adults, as a properly chosen treatment plan can correct crooked teeth at any age.

Why is it important to have straight teeth?

Correct tooth position not only improves smile aesthetics but also ensures good oral health and proper chewing. Orderly teeth help avoid caries, gum diseases, and bite problems, reduce risk of mouth injuries, and increase self-confidence.

When is Teeth Straightening Worth Choosing?

Teeth straightening is recommended when:

Crooked Teeth

Teeth are crooked or irregularly positioned

Irregular Teeth

There are gaps between teeth (diastemas) or crowded teeth

Bad Bite

Aiming to improve chewing function and bite

Aesthetics

Aesthetic smile correction is desired

For Children

Correcting growing bite and forming correct tooth line
