How to protect from caries

Prevention - best medicine

Hygiene

  • Brush teeth 2 times a day
  • Clean interdental spaces
  • Clean tongue every day
  • Change toothbrush every 3 months

Nutrition

  • Limit sweets
  • Avoid carbonated drinks
  • Eat lots of fruits, vegetables
  • Drink lots of water

Ensure your dental health on time!

Do not delay dental treatment! The earlier you notice the problem, the easier and cheaper to solve it.
