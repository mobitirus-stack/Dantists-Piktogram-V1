How is the Procedure Performed?

One visit – three stages. Everything is done as quickly and comfortably as possible.

1

Diagnostics and Extraction

We perform a 3D scan, evaluate the hone condition. If necessary, we atraumatically remove the damaged tooth or its root, preparing the place for the implant.

2

Implant Placement

We screw the implant into the same place (socket). This is done without any additional incisions, so swelling and pain after the operation are avoided.

3

Temporary Tooth

We immediately attach a temporary tooth to the implant. You leave with a full smile. After 3-4 months, it is replaced by a permanent zirconium crown.

Why Choose Immediate Implantation?

Saved Time

Requires at least twice as few visits than applying the traditional method.

Aesthetics Immediately

No need to walk without a tooth or with an uncomfortable plate during healing.

Bone Preservation

The implant naturally loads the bone, so it does not dissolve, as happens when waiting a long time after extraction.

Is This Method Suitable for You?

  • Suitable for a broken tooth
  • Suitable if tooth is untreatable
  • Sufficient bone quantity required
  • Not recommended in case of acute infection

Frequently Asked Questions

Is immediate implantation painful?

No. The procedure is performed with local anesthesia. Often it is even less painful than simple tooth extraction, because everything is done at once, leaving no open wound.

How long do such implants last?

Straumann and Nobel Biocare implants, which we use, have a lifetime warranty. With proper care, they last a lifetime.

What is the price of the procedure?

The price depends on the selected implant and the complexity of the situation. Preliminarily, implantation with a temporary crown costs about 600-800 EUR. We provide an exact estimate during the consultation.
