What is orthodontic treatment?

Correction of teeth and jaw bite

Teeth Straightening

Orthodontic treatment aims to correct teeth position and bite, as well as to form an aesthetic smile. Modern technologies allow achieving excellent results.

  • Correct teeth position
  • Correct bite
  • Restoration of facial symmetry

Health Benefits

Correctly aligned teeth not only look aesthetic but also improve general well-being and ensure long-term oral health.

  • Easier oral hygiene
  • More effective chewing
  • Lower risk of gum diseases

Straightening Methods

Choose the most suitable method for you

Metal Braces

Time-tested and effective way to straighten teeth.

Aesthetic Braces

Tooth-colored (ceramic) braces – less noticeable, therefore look more aesthetic.

Invisible Braces

Lingual braces, bonded to the inner side of the teeth – completely invisible to others.

Clear, Removable Aligners

Modern (e.g., 'Invisalign'), comfortable, and almost invisible way of straightening teeth.

Jaw Expansion

Special appliances designed to form a correct jaw arch for growing children.

Plates for Children

Removable orthodontic appliances, usually used at an early age.

Treatment by Age
