‚ùì What makes up a dental implant?

A dental implant is not just a screw. It is a system that replicates the natural tooth structure. It consists of three main parts:

  • Implant (screw) ‚Äì surgically screwed into the jaw (acts as a root).
  • Abutment ‚Äì connecting part between the implant and the crown.
  • Crown ‚Äì visible part of the tooth (zirconia or metal ceramic).
