‚ùì How Does It Work?

Instead of placing an implant for each lost tooth (which would be expensive and traumatic), only 4 implants are strategically placed in the jaw:

  • Two front implants are placed straight.
  • Two lateral implants are placed at an angle. This allows avoiding sinus lifting or nerve damage and ensures maximum stability.

A temporary but strong dental bridge is screwed onto these 4 supports on the same day. You don't walk without teeth not even for a day!
