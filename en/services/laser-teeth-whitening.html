What is Laser Whitening?

The most advanced teeth whitening technology

Laser Technology

Laser teeth whitening uses high-energy laser beams that activate a special whitening gel and quickly brighten tooth enamel.

  • Precise laser beam direction
  • Fastest whitening method
  • Minimal heat effect

Scientific Basis

Laser beams activate peroxide molecules in the whitening gel, which quickly break down and release oxygen radicals that brighten teeth.

  • Deeper penetration into enamel
  • Greater efficiency
  • Less sensitivity

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most important questions

Is the laser safe for teeth?

Yes, laser whitening is one of the safest methods. Low energy usage and temperature control ensure that the tooth structure is not damaged.

Is laser whitening painful?

The procedure is almost painless. Slight sensitivity may occur, but it passes quickly and is much weaker than with other methods.

How many times does it need to be repeated?

Usually one procedure is enough. If necessary, the procedure can be repeated after 6-12 months for maintenance or better results.
