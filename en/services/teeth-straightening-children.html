Why Early Treatment is Important?

Optimal time for teeth straightening

Right Time

First consultation with an orthodontist is recommended at 7-8 years of age. During this period it is possible to identify problems and start treatment at the optimal time.

  • 7-8 years – first consultation
  • 9-12 years – first treatment stage
  • 12-16 years – main treatment

Benefits

Early treatment allows achieving better results in a shorter time and with less effort.

  • Shorter treatment time
  • Lower costs
  • Better aesthetic result

Problems We Treat

Most tooth position and bite problems can be successfully solved

Crossbite

Upper teeth bite inside the lower teeth

Crowding

Lack of space in dental arch for correct tooth position

Spacing

Too large spaces between teeth (diastemas)

Impacted Teeth

Teeth that cannot erupt on their own

Asymmetry

mismatch of dental arch center with face midline

Malocclusion

Jaw relationship disorders

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most popular questions

When should a child visit an orthodontist?

It is recommended to visit for the first time at 7-8 years of age, even if teeth look fine. Early diagnosis allows avoiding more complex problems.

Does it hurt to put on braces?

The process is painless. There might be slight discomfort in the first days, but it passes quickly. Modern technologies minimize inconvenience.

How long does the treatment take?

Treatment duration depends on the complexity of the problem. Usually it ranges from 1 to 3 years. Early treatment can be shorter and more effective.
