Care After Straightening

How to maintain the result

Retainers

It is necessary to wear retainers after straightening to preserve the result.

  • Constant for the first 6 months
  • Then only at nights
  • For life

Hygiene

Dental hygiene is especially important during and after straightening.

  • Gentle brushing
  • Flossing
  • Regular visits
