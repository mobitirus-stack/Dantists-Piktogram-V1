Frequently Asked Questions

We use modern anesthesia, so the procedure is painless. After procedure there might be slight sensitivity for 2-3 days.

It is recommended to wait 2 hours after procedure before eating. For the first days eat soft, cool food.

Usually the wound starts healing after 7-10 days. Full gum and bone healing takes 4-6 weeks.

Usually after simple extraction you can return to work the next day. After complex procedures longer rest is recommended.

Care After Extraction

How to take care of your mouth after procedure

What To Do

  • Keep gauze pad for 30-60 min
  • Use ice packs
  • Drink lots of fluids
  • Brush teeth carefully
  • Take medications as prescribed

What To Avoid

  • Do not drink hot drinks
  • Do not smoke for 48 hours
  • Do not drink alcohol
  • Do not eat hard food
  • Do not rinse mouth intensively
