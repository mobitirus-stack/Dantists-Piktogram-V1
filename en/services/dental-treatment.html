Our Dental Treatment Services

We offer a full spectrum of dental treatment services, using the most advanced technologies and highest quality materials to ensure the best possible result.

Conservative Treatment

Treatment of dental damage using filling methods, preserving the maximum amount of healthy tissue.

  • Aesthetic filling
  • Composite materials
  • Color matching

Endodontic Treatment

Root canal treatment under a microscope, allowing to save a tooth even with complex damage.

  • Microscopic treatment
  • Complete canal cleaning
  • 3D diagnostics

Surgical Treatment

Solving complex cases with surgical methods when conservative treatment is no longer effective.

  • Tooth extraction
  • Wisdom tooth extraction
  • Fast healing

Pediatric Dentistry

Children's dental treatment in a friendly environment, forming a positive attitude towards dentistry.

  • Treatment of damaged teeth (after trauma or due to nerve death)
  • Dental sealants
  • Preventive programs

Prosthodontics

Tooth restoration with the help of prostheses, restoring functionality and aesthetics.

  • Removable dentures
  • Fixed bridges
  • Crowns

Frequently Asked Questions

We answer the most popular questions about dental treatment so you feel confident and calm.

Modern dentistry uses effective anesthesia, so most procedures are painless. We use a computerized anesthesia system ensuring precise drug delivery and minimal discomfort. Slight sensitivity may occur after the procedure but passes quickly.

It is recommended to visit the dentist every 6-12 months for a preventive check-up and professional cleaning. Such frequency allows detecting problems early and avoiding complex treatment. If you feel pain or other symptoms, contact us immediately.

Night toothache can be due to various reasons: caries, infection, gum diseases or bruxism. First, you can take a painkiller and apply a cold compress. However, it is important to contact a dentist as soon as possible, as night pain often indicates an advanced problem.

Treatment duration depends on the complexity of the problem. Simple filling takes 30-60 minutes, root canal treatment - 1-2 hours, more complex procedures may require several visits. During consultation, the doctor will determine the exact treatment plan and duration.

Yes, part of dental services is compensated from the Compulsory Health Insurance Fund (PSDF). Preventive services for children under 18, pregnant women, and part of adult services (e.g., when the price exceeds 120€) are compensated. We will provide detailed information during consultation.
