Important Information About Insurance Payment

Everything you need to know about health insurance services in our clinic

Insurance Contract and Service List

You can find the list of services paid by the insurance company in your insurance contract. Each insurance company sets its own limits and frames for paid services, so it is necessary to familiarize yourself with the terms of your contract in advance.

Employee Health Insurance

For clients with employee health insurance, some services may be provided free of charge. This depends on your health insurance package. Most corporate insurance plans cover preventive visits, professional oral hygiene, and basic treatment.

Self-payment

It is important to note that for healthcare services not covered by insurance, payment is the responsibility of the patient. We recommend consulting with your insurance advisor whether the specific services you need will be paid for under your insurance contract. "Švytintys Dantys" is not responsible for insurance companies' decisions not to pay for provided services.

Cooperation and Documentation

For your convenience, we cooperate with private health insurance companies – this way services can be obtained faster and more conveniently. If we do not have direct cooperation with your insurance company, after the service is provided we can provide a medical report and an invoice, which you can submit to the insurance.
